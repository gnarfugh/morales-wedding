FAQ

+When is the wedding date?

Saturday October 14, 2017

+When is the RSVP date?

TBD, you’ll receive it with your invitation 6-8 weeks before the wedding.

+What time should I arrive?

Ceremony is at 5, so around 4:30-4:45 would be ideal so you can be seated.

+What is the parking situation?

You have a few options. There is two-hour metered parking in front of the venue. Alternatively, there’s a parking garage right across the street from the venue that offers parking at $1/hour. Street parking is free on Sundays. If you have parking at your hotel, you can request a taxi or utilize Uber/Lyft to make it to the venue.

+What do I wear?

The event is formal but laidback. Anything you wish to wear that is in good taste would be okay.

+Where is the ceremony?

It will be held at Great Hall at Best Place – Historic Pabst Brewery.

+Where is the reception?

Same location. You’ll head downstairs to the speakeasy for drinks while the room is being reset.

+Will alcohol be served?

Absolutely.

+I am from out of town/do not want to drive home afterwards. What accommodations are available?

Please visit the Hotels section of the website for all details and make your reservation asap! Hotels can only offer a certain amount of rooms at the discounted rate, but the earlier they book up the more discounted rooms they can add.

+What can I expect, generally, of the evening?

Ceremony, drinks, dinner, drinks, dancing, drinks

+Can I take pictures?

Of course! All we ask is that you try to avoid blocking our photographer. You can also share any photos with us through social media with #MorAlesInMilwaukee

+What else is there to do when you aren't getting married?

Of course! We hope that you can make the most of your trip there and visit some nearby attractions. A good place to start is http://www.visitmilwaukee.org/visitors/attractions.

+Can I take home centerpieces, decorations, etc?

No. Some of the decorations are borrowed or we are hoping to reuse so please on leave our reception with a full belly and memories of the evening.

+I have dietary restrictions - what should I do?

It’s a buffet, so you can choose your meal as you please. If you have any big concerns, please reach out.

+What do I do if something changes and I can/can't make it after I have RSVP’d?

We understand things come up. Just email us right away .
